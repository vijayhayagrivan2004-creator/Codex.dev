— FAQ

Frequently Asked Questions

Have questions? Here are answers to the most common ones. Can't find what you're looking for? Just send me a message.

How long does it take to build a website?

It depends on the project scope. A simple landing page takes 3-5 days. A full multi-page business website typically takes 2-4 weeks. I'll give you a clear timeline during our initial consultation so you always know what to expect.

Do I need to provide the content and images?

Ideally, yes. Nobody knows your business better than you! However, if you need help, I can provide stock images and recommend copywriters.

Will my website be mobile-friendly?

Absolutely! Every website I build is 100% responsive and tested across various devices.

What technologies do you use?

I primarily use HTML, CSS, JavaScript, and modern frameworks depending on your specific needs.

How do payments work?

I typically require a 50% upfront deposit to secure your spot and start the project, with the remaining 50% due before final launch.

Do you offer post-launch support?

Yes, I offer maintenance packages and a standard bug-fix period immediately following launch.
