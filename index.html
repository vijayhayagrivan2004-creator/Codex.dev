FAQ

Frequently Asked Questions

Have questions? Here are answers to the most common ones. Can't find what you're looking for? Just send me a message.

How long does it take to build a website?

It depends on the project scope. A simple landing page takes 3–5 days. A full multi-page business website typically takes 2–4 weeks. I'll give you a clear timeline during our initial consultation so you always know what to expect.

Do I need to provide the content and images?

Ideally, yes — you know your business best! However, I can help guide you on what content is needed and can use high-quality stock photos as placeholders until you have your own.

Will my website be mobile-friendly?

Absolutely — 100% guaranteed. Every website I build is designed with a mobile-first mindset and rigorously tested across different screen sizes and browsers, including iOS and Android devices.

What technologies do you use?

I primarily work with pure HTML5, CSS3, and vanilla JavaScript for clean and fast websites. I always choose the right technology for the project rather than overengineering it.

How do payments work?

I typically work with a 50% upfront payment to begin work, and the remaining 50% upon project completion and your approval. I accept UPI, bank transfer, and other convenient methods.

Do you offer post-launch support?

Yes! All plans come with a free revision period post-launch. After that, I offer affordable ongoing maintenance packages for regular updates, security checks, and content changes.
